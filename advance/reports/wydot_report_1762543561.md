# WYDOT Answer Report

**Question:** tell me about wydot

**Answer:**

The Wyoming Department of Transportation (WYDOT) is the state agency responsible for road and bridge construction in Wyoming. They express appreciation to their employees, the Federal Highway Administration, and the Associated General Contractors of Wyoming for their help in producing the Standard Specifications for Road and Bridge Construction. WYDOT works with several internal programs including the Construction Office, Bridge, Geology, Highway Development, Contracts & Estimates, Environmental Services, Maintenance, Traffic, Materials, and District Construction and Maintenance programs. The Director of WYDOT is K. Luke Reiner.
